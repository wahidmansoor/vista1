### Formulas
#### Mosteller Formula
- BSA (m²) = √(height(cm) × weight(kg) / 3600)
- Most commonly used
- Validated across populations

#### DuBois Formula
- BSA = 0.007184 × height(cm)^0.725 × weight(kg)^0.425
- Historical standard
- Known limitations

### Clinical Applications
- Chemotherapy dosing
- Clinical trial calculations
- Pediatric considerations
- Obesity adjustments
